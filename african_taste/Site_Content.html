01 Brainstorm And Write

With your topics and audience in mind, please write at least 600 words on your website idea providing text content that your audience may want to read or get from your website. You can just freewrite any pertinent information about your website idea following good paragraph and sentence structure, thought processes, and so on. You may also consider answering some or all of the following questions about your website idea:

  • What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site?
  • What information is necessary to complete any actions your site offers?
  • Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions?
